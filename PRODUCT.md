# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Friend groups and social circles deciding together — what movie to watch, what book to read next, what to do. Used casually in group chats, shared via link. Participants join on phones or laptops, often in a live session together.

## Product Purpose

A lightweight group polling tool that makes collective decisions fun. Create a poll, share a link, nominate options, vote, see the winner. The whole flow should feel like a game, not a form.

## Brand Personality

Playful, lively, together. The energy of a group Discord server — organized but not corporate, social but not chaotic. Like the group chat that actually makes a decision.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS: navy + white + rounded cards, dashboard clichés, "hero metric" layouts
- Sterile form tools (Google Forms, Typeform clones)
- Bland productivity aesthetic — no cream/sand/linen backgrounds, no eyebrow text on every section
- Flat or gray palettes with no personality

## Design Principles

1. **Feels like a game, not a chore** — every interaction should have energy; waiting and voting should feel alive
2. **The group is the product** — participant names, counts, and activity are first-class UI elements
3. **Dark and vivid** — Discord/Slack-like dark UI with a strong violet accent; the Dither background is the atmosphere
4. **Playful structure** — organized like Slack (clear phases, clear hierarchy) but warmer, with more personality in motion and color
5. **Mobile-first social** — most users will be on their phones in the middle of a group decision

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum; strong contrast against dark backgrounds
- Reduced motion support required (Dither animation + any UI transitions)
- Touch-friendly tap targets (44px minimum)
